Important Covid-19 Information Warning

Updated June 4th 2020

The City of Pickering has now allowed the courts to be opened starting May 31st 2020. There are COVID-19 rules and restrictions that need to be followed.

The following rules must be obeyed at all times:

  • No Double Play
  • No Guests
  • No group Lessons
  • No Leagues
  • Usage of portable toiletes are not allowed
  • Social distancing, hand hygiene & disinfecting surfaces will be required
  • Players must log their cours usage
