Court Rules

The Courts are Yours! Remember, it is the responsibility of each member to take care of our courts and to set a positive example for others in the community.

General Rules

  1. You must be a current Amberlea Tennis Club member to use the courts
  2. Club events (league play, lessons, etc.) will pre-empt general play. Please check the court calendar

Court usage rules to be communicated later.

How Does Court Usage Work?

NEW Court RULES: in order to make the rule regarding when one can play and the duration of play as simple and as clear as possible, the following new rule will apply:

  • time allotted for play is 1 (one) hour per session
  • the 1 hour start/ends either on the hour or on the half hour.

Examples:

  1. I start playing at 1pm. I need to leave the court at 2pm
  2. I start playing at 1:15pm. I need to leave the court at 2pm.
  3. I start playing at 12:30pm. I need to leave the court at 1:30pm
  4. I start playing at 12:45pm, I need to leave the court by 1:30pm

The court with the gate will be the “hour” court; the one closest to the soccer fields, the “half hour” court.

Also, there will be a “bonus” period of 5 minutes. This means:

  • If I arrive at 12:45 and the courts are empty, I start playing. At 12:55, people show up to play at 1pm.
  • Unless a compromise can be reached by all players concerned, the people arriving at 12:55 should get priority to the courts at 1pm.

There will need to be honesty and fair-play by all members, especially for those who are waiting to play.

Code of Conduct

Amberlea Tennis Club strives to provide a safe, welcoming and positive environment/ atmosphere for the members, programs and facilities.

It is a club for all players regardless of playing ability, and the focus is on recreational tennis and having fun.

The courts, including the practice court, are to be used for their intended purpose, namely playing tennis and nothing else.

Active members are allowed to play with guests up to 3 times a season without a membership. After that, guests are strongly encouraged to join.

Given the diversity of the membership and the location of the courts, i.e. in Shadybrook Park and in close proximity to a school and playground, all members and guests are expected to conduct themselves on and off the courts in a dignified manner and display sportsmanship at all times. The goal is to encourage a community in which individuals are treated with respect, dignity and fairness.

The use of profanities, threatening language and/or violent gestures towards anyone as well as racquet abuse will not be tolerated.

The Executive takes the above very seriously and any violation of these as well as the court rules will be reviewed on a case-by-case basis. Depending on the severity, actions can range from a written warning to the expulsion from the club.

Note: Amberlea Tennis Club and its members and guests will also abide by the City of Pickering’s “Zero Tolerance Policy” at all times.
