Drop-In Program

This free two week program introduces participants to the game of tennis.

Catalin Codita (Club Pro)

A former top national junior player from Romania with over 20 years of teaching/coaching tennis experience. Founder and head coach of Littleace-Tennis school, a grass-roots tennis school operating in Durham Region and offering introductory and high performance programs for juniors of all ages. Catalin and his team also offers club programs for adults of all levels of play.

Catalin and his team run the largest Summer tennis camp in Durham under the global brand Nike Sportscamps.

Catalin is Tennis Canada certified club pro, High-performance Tennis pro certified by Romanian Tennis Federation, ITF Certified on-court assessor. Member of Tennis Professional Association, Professional Tennis Registry and GPTCA (Global Professional Tennis Coach Association). Former Vice President junior development for Scarborough Tennis Federation.

Catalin can be contacted via email (catalin@littleace.ca) or phone (416-624-4880)
